## 2025-05-24 - Matplotlib Object Lifecycle Overhead   **Learning:** The continuous instantiation and destruction of `plt.figure()` inside loops during AlphaFold metrics generation (specifically `run_bolt_analysis_cycle.py`) consumes roughly 50-60% of total CPU execution time due to internal rendering setup, canvas allocation, and font loading overhead.   **Action:** Replaced iterative figure creation with static `fig, ax` initialization outside the loop, dynamically updating data via `line.set_data()` (for static-length arrays) and `ax.clear()` (for variable-shape PAE matrices) to bypass figure recreation.
