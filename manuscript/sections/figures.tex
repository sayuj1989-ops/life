This high-gain instability is consistent with the loss of voltage-dependent gain control in Piezo2 sensors~\cite{sanchez2024piezo}. Furthermore, the 'Inflamed Torsion' pathway—driven by unchecked MMP1/3 activity in the absence of gravitational loading~\cite{chen2025mmp}—degrades torsional stiffness ($GJ$), effectively lowering the critical buckling threshold and locking the spine into this scoliotic state.}
