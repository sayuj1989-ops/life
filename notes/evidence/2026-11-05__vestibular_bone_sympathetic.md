# Evidence Note: The Gravity-Bone Axis

## Claim
The vestibular system, specifically the otolith organs (gravity sensors), exerts a direct regulatory control over bone mass and microarchitecture via the sympathetic nervous system (SNS), independent of physical loading or muscle activity.

## Mechanism
Vignaux et al. (2013) demonstrate a neural pathway linking gravity sensing to skeletal maintenance:
1.  **Sensor:** Otoliths detect gravitational acceleration.
2.  **Relay:** Vestibular Nuclei project to the sympathetic chain.
3.  **Effector:** Sympathetic nerves release norepinephrine acting on **Beta-2 adrenergic receptors** on osteoblasts.
4.  **Outcome:** High sympathetic tone (stress/mismatch) inhibits osteoblasts and activates osteoclasts (via RANKL), leading to bone loss. Bilateral vestibular lesion (BVL) in rats causes significant bone loss in weight-bearing bones, which is prevented by the beta-blocker **Propranolol**.

## Relevance to S-Shaped Spinal Growth / Scoliosis
This establishes a **"Neural-Material" bridge** in scoliosis etiology:
- **Asymmetry:** If a patient has asymmetric vestibular function (otolith imbalance), this would drive **asymmetric sympathetic outflow** to the spine.
- **Wedging:** The side with higher sympathetic tone would experience reduced bone formation (osteopenia), potentially leading to the vertebral wedging characteristic of scoliosis progression.
- **Microgravity:** The rapid bone loss in spaceflight might not be solely due to lack of mechanical load (Wolff's Law) but due to the "silencing" of this tonic vestibular input, or a stress-response surge in sympathetic tone due to sensory conflict.

## Support for Counter-Curvature Hypothesis
The "Biological Counter-Curvature" theory posits that the spine's geometry is an informational readout of the organism's gravitational model.
- **Gravity as a Morphogen:** This finding proves that "Gravity" is not just a mechanical force but a **neural signal** that actively tunes material properties ($E$).
- **Counter-Curvature:** If the internal model of gravity is tilted (vestibular asymmetry), the "Material Controller" (SNS) will remodel the spine to match this tilted reality, creating a physical curvature to compensate for a sensory error.
