### Key findings/decisions:
* Started Phase 1 (Literature Review — Decomposing τ).
* Reviewed literature on the components of the proprioceptive delay $\tau$, specifically breaking it down into: peripheral transduction, afferent conduction, spinal relay, cerebellar processing, efferent conduction, neuromuscular junction (NMJ), and electromechanical delay (EMD).
* Based on literature evidence (e.g., Peterka 2002 framework, unverified — needs literature confirmation), created an initial "$\tau$ budget".
* Identified total $\tau$ delay is the sum of these sequential biological latencies. Typical values in posture control (Peterka) are ~150-200ms, heavily influenced by neural conduction and electromechanical delays.
