# Hydraulic Buckling Experiment Report

**Date:** 2026-03-09 05:38:11

## Hypothesis: The Inflamed Torsion Pathway
Microgravity leads to hydraulic stagnation (swelling), which degrades annular fibers.
This reduction in Torsional Stiffness ($GJ$) causes the spine to buckle under active muscle tone.

## Proprioceptive Mismatch
The 'Mismatch Energy' represents the difference between the actual elastic energy stored in the soft rod and the energy the organism 'expects' (based on a healthy stiffness reference).
A large positive mismatch indicates the system is 'softer than expected', leading to gain errors.
