### Hypothesized Mechanical Role: "The Anisotropic Lever"
The structural convergence of membrane sensors (PIEZO) and nuclear resistors (LMNA) into this high-anisotropy cluster suggests that **aspect ratio is a functional proxy for mechanosensitivity**.

- **Mechanism:** Extended structures maximize the torque or bending moment generated by a given force field (e.g., gravity-induced shear or membrane tension). A spherical sensor would minimize this interaction cross-section.
- **Fail State:** If mutations (or unloading) reduce the effective anisotropy (e.g., via domain collapse or loss of tension-dependent unfolding), the "Lever Arm" shortens, and the sensor becomes "deaf" to the weak signal of gravity.
