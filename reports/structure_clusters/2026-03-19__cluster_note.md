Their high domain blockiness allows them to act as modular shock-absorbers that transmit force without rupturing under physiological gravity loads, preserving muscle tone and spinal alignment. The metabolic regulators in this cluster (e.g., PPARGC1A) ensure that this continuous mechanical demand is matched by the necessary thermodynamic energy supply.
