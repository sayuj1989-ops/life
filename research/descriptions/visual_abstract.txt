Visual Abstract: Biological Countercurvature of Spacetime

Core Concept: Living systems use developmental information to warp their local effective geometry, creating a "counter-curvature" that neutralizes the deformation caused by gravity.

Visual Metaphor:
1. **Gravity**: Represented as a standard "spacetime grid" curving downwards (like a gravity well).
2. **Biology**: A glowing, structured spine (S-curve) growing upwards, defying the gravitational pull.
3. **Information**: A field of "counter-curvature" (purple/blue energy) surrounding the spine, pushing back against the gravity grid, effectively flattening the local spacetime or creating an upward "hill" for the structure to inhabit.

Key Elements:
- Contrast between the heavy, downward pull of Gravity (grey/orange) and the upward, organizing force of Information (blue/purple).
- The spine is the interface where these two metrics collide and balance.
