Title: Biological Countercurvature Visual Abstract

Concept: A dramatic, futuristic, and high-impact 3D conceptual rendering of the Biological Countercurvature of Spacetime theory. The visual abstract demonstrates the spine acting as a standing wave of biological information counteracting gravity.

Main Subject: In the center, a human spine exhibiting a perfect S-curve (lordosis and kyphosis). However, it is not a normal bone structure. It is rendered as a brilliant, glowing lattice of golden "Information" threads, reminiscent of fiber optics or a directed energy field.

Environment: The spine exists within a dark, deep-space void featuring a pronounced, warped spacetime grid. The grid bends dramatically, simulating a deep gravitational well pulling downwards.

Forces in Opposition:
- Gravity: Depicted by heavy, ominous dark purple or indigo arrows pushing relentlessly downward from above, representing the "Gravitational Paradox" and mechanical loading.
- Countercurvature (Information): The glowing golden spine actively radiates energy outward and upward, pushing back against the dark gravitational arrows. The golden energy smooths out the warped spacetime grid immediately surrounding the spine, showing its ability to locally flatten or resist spacetime curvature.

Text/Annotation: A subtle, glowing equation or text reading "Information = Geometry" should be integrated into the energy field near the spine.

Style: Photorealistic, cinematic 3D scientific illustration. The high contrast between the luminous gold of the biological information and the oppressive dark void of gravity is essential. Designed for a keynote presentation or a major journal cover.
