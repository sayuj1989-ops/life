Golden threads or streams of light flow along the spine, representing "developmental information" (HOX patterning and IEC) acting as the active biological countercurvature.
The background is dark and cosmic, emphasizing the fundamental, physical nature of the theory. The overall aesthetic should be highly polished, cinematic, and suitable for a keynote presentation or journal cover.
