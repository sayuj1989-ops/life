Title: Biological Countercurvature Visual Abstract

Concept: A striking, cyber-biological rendering of the human spine acting as a standing wave of information that counteracts gravitational spacetime curvature.

Setting: A dark, ethereal void featuring a warped spacetime grid (like a gravity well), visually illustrating the concept of "Spacetime". The grid should be warped by the spine.

Subject: The spine is the central element, forming a perfect S-curve (lordosis and kyphosis). It is not a standard skeletal bone model, but a glowing, translucent structure built of intricate, golden "Information" threads (resembling fiber-optic data streams or DNA).

Forces:
- Gravity: Represented by heavy, dark purple or dark blue vertical arrows pushing downward, trying to buckle the spine.
- Information (Countercurvature): Represented by the golden light radiating outwards and upwards from the spine, actively pushing back against the dark arrows to maintain its optimal geometry.

Annotation/Text: "Information = Geometry" subtly integrated or glowing near the spine.

Style: High-end, 3D rendered scientific illustration. Highly impactful, suitable for a keynote presentation or journal cover. The contrast between the glowing golden "Countercurvature" and the dark, heavy "Gravity" must be visually striking.
