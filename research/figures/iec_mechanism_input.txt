A diagram illustrating the Information-Elasticity Coupling (IEC) mechanism. On the left, a gradient field labeled 'Information I(s)' (represented as a color gradient from blue to red). This field feeds into three parallel pathways: 1) 'Target Curvature Bias' (modifying kappa_target), 2) 'Constitutive Bias' (modifying Stiffness E), and 3) 'Active Moment' (M_active). These pathways converge on a 3D rod structure (Cosserat Rod) on the right, causing it to bend into a specific shape (e.g., an S-curve). Arrows indicate the flow of information into mechanical properties.
