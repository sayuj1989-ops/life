A detailed conceptual diagram of the Information-Elasticity Coupling (IEC) feedback loop in spinal development. The visual shows a cycle: Gravity exerts force on the developing spine (Cosserat rod). Mechanosensory cells detect this stress, transmitting signals to gene regulatory networks (HOX patterning). These networks output an 'anisotropic supply' (developmental information). This information couples with the mechanical structure, creating a countercurvature that pushes back against gravity, stabilizing the spine.
