A high-impact visual abstract for the theory of 'Biological Countercurvature'.
Central image: A human spine where the vertebrae are depicted as discrete geometric manifolds.
Background: A warped grid representing spacetime, showing how biological information (genes) curves the space to counteract gravity.
Concept: Gravity pulls down (red arrow), Information pulls up/corrects (blue arrow).
The spine is an active 'standing wave' against the flow of gravity.
Style: Scientific, clean, 3D render style, similar to Nature/Science covers.
