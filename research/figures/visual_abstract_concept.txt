Visual Abstract: Biological Countercurvature of Spacetime.

A conceptual illustration combining biological growth with relativistic geometry.

Key Elements:
1. **The Grid**: A 3D metric grid (representing spacetime/information field) that is warped and curved. It should look like a gravity well or a complex manifold.
2. **The Spine**: A human spine growing within this grid. The spine's curvature perfectly matches the warped grid lines, showing that it is following the "path of least resistance" (geodesic) in this curved space.
3. **Contrast**: On one side, a straight grid with a straight spine (Healthy/Euclidean). On the other, the warped grid with a scoliotic spine (Pathological/Riemannian).
4. **Style**: High-end scientific illustration, 3D render style, translucent materials, glowing grid lines. Blue and gold color scheme.

Caption: "Scoliosis as a geometric adaptation to a warped information field."
