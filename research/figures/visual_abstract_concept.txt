Visual Abstract: Biological Countercurvature of Spacetime.
A 3D visualization of a human spine growing within a warped metric grid representing spacetime and developmental information.
- The spine is glowing and follows a curved path of least resistance (geodesic).
- The grid is distorted by "mass" (genetic information), similar to general relativity.
- Mathematical equations (e.g., R_deficit, D/Dt) are subtly overlaid on the grid.
- Background: A deep blue starry cosmos merging with biological tissue textures.
- The spine shows a transition from a straight line to an S-shape as it navigates the curved space.
- Style: High-impact Nature/Science cover art, photorealistic yet conceptual, cinematic lighting.
