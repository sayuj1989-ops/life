A 3D visualization of a biological rod (spine) growing in a curved spacetime metric.
The grid lines of space are curved like a gravity well, but the spine remains straight by actively fighting the curvature.
The 'shadow' of the spine on the flat ground is curved (scoliosis), but the spine itself is straight in its effective geometry.
High contrast, scientific style, blue and gold color scheme.
Label: "Biological Countercurvature"
