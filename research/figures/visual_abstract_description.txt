Visual Abstract: Biological Countercurvature.
A high-quality 3D scientific illustration showing a biological structure (like a vertebrate spine) maintaining an upright S-shape.
The key concept is that the organism modifies the local spacetime metric to fight gravity.
Visual elements:
1. The Spine: Detailed, organic, glowing slightly.
2. The Metric: A 3D grid or mesh surrounding the spine. This grid should be visibly curved or warped around the spine, representing the "countercurvature".
3. Gravity: Represented as a uniform vertical field (faint lines).
4. The warping of the grid should visually suggest it is supporting the spine against gravity.
Style: Nature/Science journal cover art. Realistic but conceptual.
