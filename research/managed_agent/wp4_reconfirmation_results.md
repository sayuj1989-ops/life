## Method

1. Fetched AlphaFold-predicted structures for 31 proteins via the AlphaFold EBI API.
2. Parsed Cα coordinates from each PDB file.
3. Computed the gyration tensor: G_jk = (1/N) Σ_i (r_i - r̄)_j (r_i - r̄)_k
4. Extracted eigenvalues λ₁ ≥ λ₂ ≥ λ₃ and computed anisotropy = √(λ₁/λ₃).
5. Ran one-sided Mann-Whitney U test (DEMAND > SUPPLY).
6. Computed Cohen's d (pooled SD) and Cliff's delta effect sizes.
