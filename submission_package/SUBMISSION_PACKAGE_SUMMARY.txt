================================================================================
                        ✨ WHAT MAKES YOUR WORK UNIQUE
================================================================================

THE PROBLEM:
→ Spinal S-curves are universal across vertebrates, yet their developmental
  origin is poorly understood. Current models (passive geodesics) fail to
  explain zero-gravity observations and scoliotic deviations.

YOUR SOLUTION:
→ Biological Countercurvature: Developmental information (HOX genes) acts
  as a geometric modifier of tissue elasticity, creating Information-
  Elasticity Coupling (IEC) that generates the S-curve independent of gravity.

KEY FINDINGS:
1. Phase diagram: Three regimes (gravity-dominated, cooperative, information-dominated)
2. Cross-species validation: 9 mammalian species fit the model
3. Scoliosis predictions: Mutations correlate with IEC disruptions
4. Microgravity prediction: Model predicts spine changes in zero-gravity

WHY NATURE WILL BE INTERESTED:
✓ Novel theoretical framework bridging physics + biology
✓ Testable predictions (especially microgravity)
✓ Cross-species validation
✓ Clinical applications (scoliosis treatment)
✓ Reproducible code + open science
✓ Addresses 100+ year old biological mystery
